LetFlix is a website that is used to stream movies from various countries, whether it's cinema films, series films, or animated films. LetFlix was first established in 2021 to be precise on May 10, 2021. Due to the pandemic, cinemas all over the world had to close temporarily and it made many people unable to watch cinemas anymore. Hence, LetFlix wants to provide the cinema experience by supplying many interesting films in one application.
